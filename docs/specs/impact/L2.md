# IMPACT — Impact: L2 Requirements

Detailed requirements for the [Impact capability](./L1.md). Each requirement traces to one L1 in this capability. Acceptance criteria are in Given/When/Then form; acceptance test files must carry a `Traces to: L2-IMPACT-###` comment header.

---

## L2-IMPACT-001: Relationship-oriented impact metrics
**Traces to:** L1-IMPACT-001
**Source:** mocks (deferred)

The Demonstrate screen presents metrics framed around relationships and duration, not shipping
speed (e.g. weeks walked alongside, meals shared, volunteers still serving, stories of change).

**Acceptance Criteria:**
1. Given a project in Demonstrate, when the screen loads, then it shows relationship-oriented stat tiles (e.g. "weeks walked alongside", "stories of change").
2. Given the screen, when it renders, then the framing headline conveys "Impact is friendship, compounded by time."


## L2-IMPACT-002: Longitudinal story timeline
**Traces to:** L1-IMPACT-001
**Source:** mocks (deferred)

Demonstrate shows a time-ordered story of change over successive weeks.

**Acceptance Criteria:**
1. Given recorded stories, when the timeline renders, then entries appear in chronological (week) order.


## L2-IMPACT-003: Rejoice gratitude wall
**Traces to:** L1-IMPACT-001
**Source:** mocks (deferred)

Demonstrate surfaces the 5R "Rejoice" thanksgivings as a wall of gratitude quotes.

**Acceptance Criteria:**
1. Given logged thanksgivings, when the gratitude wall renders, then each is shown as an attributed quote card.
