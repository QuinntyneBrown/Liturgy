Co-creation with the Holy Spirit

Warm-handoff script when a caller is in danger

One work item, five movements. Liturgy holds the card open until each is logged — Request, Receive, Review, Render, Rejoice — then it may be marked Done.

"Commit your work to the Lord, and your plans will be established." Proverbs 16:3

1
Request

Invite the Spirit into the work

Name what you're seeking before you start. A short, honest ask.

Logged

"Show us how to protect a caller without frightening them further."

2
Receive

Wait, and write down what comes

Capture the ideas, nudges, and constraints that surface — without editing them yet.

Logged

Lead with the caller's consent · keep the volunteer visible · never transfer silently · a human voice stays on the line.

3
Review

Synthesize what emerged

Shape the raw ideas into a direction the team can build.

Logged

A three-step spoken script + a visible "staying with you" indicator, reviewed with two volunteers.

4
Render · current

Build toward what you saw

Make it real. Link the work, note what changed from the vision, and log the movement to continue.

5
Rejoice

Give thanks for what was made

Name the good in what came about — and who it serves. Rejoice closes the loop.

Render and Rejoice remain. Done unlocks when all five are logged.
