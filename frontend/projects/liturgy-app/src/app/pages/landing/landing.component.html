The 4D Cycle Every project moves through four movements — in order.
01 — Discover
See clearly

Reorient through the lens of Christ. Lament. Understand who the problem touches most.

02 — Discern
Choose wisely

Seek God's wisdom, then decide: Reject, Receive, Reimagine, or Create.

03 — Develop
Co-create

Build in sprints while running the 5R loop — Request, Receive, Review, Render, Rejoice.

04 — Demonstrate
Measure love

Redefine impact as friendship compounded by time, not speed shipped.

Inside Develop
